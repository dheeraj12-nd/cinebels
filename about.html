Your trusted partner in home entertainment

From our exclusive lineup of legendary audio brands to personalized consultations, we are here to deliver the best sound experience for your money

We’re the experts in home cinema and high-end audio solutions

Cinebels is the product of the vision and expertise of Cinerama Pvt Ltd, a renowned name in professional cinema sound and projection equipment in India with over six decades of experience. Over the years, we've become known for our expertise, exclusivity, and class as the exclusive distributor for the world’s most legendary audio brands including Klipsch, Onkyo, Pioneer, Jamo, and Integra.

We are passionate about bringing cinematic experiences to life. Our love for audio runs deep, and we believe that sound is a deeply personal experience that has the power to transform the way we interact with the world around us.

But what really sets us apart is our commitment to a personalized approach. We believe that audio is more than just sound waves — it's an emotional journey that should be tailored to your individual preferences. Hence, our team takes the time and effort to understand your unique needs and lifestyle to help you find the audio solution that's perfect for you.

When you walk into our state-of-the-art showrooms, you will find multiple experience zones that allow you to fully immerse yourself in the audio experience. Trust us when we say that we provide epic sound that you will cherish for years to come.

Born with a vision

Driven by our mission

Providing the best audio and video solution for a given price point from the world’s best brands.
